Frequently Asked Questions

While our fruit and cream pies do not contain nuts as an ingredient, they are prepared in a kitchen where nuts and nut products are handled. As a result, there is a risk of cross-contact. We take food allergies seriously and follow standard cleaning and preparation procedures; however, we cannot guarantee that our pies are completely free from trace amounts of nuts.

For detailed ingredient and allergen information, feel free to contact us — we’re happy to provide an up-to-date copy of our ingredient and allergen information sheet upon request.
Our fruit and nut pies are best enjoyed at room temperature for optimal flavor and texture. Pies made with whipped cream, meringue, or custard should be kept refrigerated until you're ready to serve them. While our pies are freshest on the day of purchase, they can be stored in the refrigerator if you plan to enjoy them later. Refrigeration helps preserve their quality and taste.
You can reheat our fruit and nut pies in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C). For best results, cover the pie loosely with aluminum foil to ensure even warming and to prevent it from drying out. Place it on a baking sheet or directly on the oven rack — whichever you prefer — and heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
