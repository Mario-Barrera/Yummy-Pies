Delivery Policy

Yummy Pies LLC guarantees delivery to the address specified by the customer and/or recipient at the time of ordering, for the scheduled date of delivery.

It is the customer's responsibility to ensure the accuracy of the delivery address. While we may correct minor typos (such as in the ZIP code), we cannot guarantee correction of major errors like incorrect street names, numbers, cities, or apartment/suite/unit details.

No refunds or store credit will be issued for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer or recipient.

A delivery time window reminder will be sent via text message the day before the scheduled delivery. This is an estimate, not a guaranteed time. Our drivers will make every effort to deliver on time and will notify customers of any delays due to factors like weather, traffic, construction, or vehicle issues.

Yummy Pies LLC reserves the right to modify this delivery policy at any time.
