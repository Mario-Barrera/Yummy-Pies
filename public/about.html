About Yummy Pies

Welcome to Yummy Pies — where pie isn’t just dessert, it’s a full-blown flavor celebration.

Founded in 2021 by Mario Barrera, a graduate of The Art Institute of Austin, Yummy Pies was born from a lifelong passion for baking. Mario’s love for pies began at age 10, baking alongside his mom in the family kitchen. What started as a cherished tradition quickly became a mission: to create pies that leave a lasting impression from the very first bite.

What’s our secret? The crust. Flaky, tender, and irresistibly buttery — it’s the foundation of every unforgettable pie. We pour love and precision into each golden layer, because without the perfect crust, the pie just isn’t complete. And that’s just the beginning. Our fillings burst with bold, fresh flavors from comforting classics to daring seasonal creations.

At Yummy Pies, we’re not just baking — we’re creating moments. Moments of joy, comfort, and just the right amount of indulgence. Ready to find your new favorite slice? Let the pie adventure begin.

— Mario Barrera
